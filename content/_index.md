**Epimethean Challenge (EC)** is a 4X, play-by-email, science-fantasy game of
exploration, expansion, exploitation, and extermination among the stars.

This site is the complete documentation for players and referees, organized with
the [Diátaxis](https://diataxis.fr) framework so you can find the right kind of
help for what you are trying to do.

## New to Epimethean Challenge?

If you have never played EC, begin with the [tutorials](tutorials). They assume
no prior knowledge and walk you from signing up through submitting your first
turn by email.
