## Before you begin

You will need:

- A working email address (EC is played entirely by email).
- A plain-text editor for composing your turn orders.

## Steps

### 1. Join a game

Request a position in an open game. You will receive a welcome email confirming
your empire, your home system, and the turn schedule.

### 2. Read your first turn packet

Each turn, the game emails you a report describing what your empire can see and
do. Open it and skim the sections: your systems, your fleets, and the orders you
may issue.

### 3. Prepare to give orders

Orders are written as plain text and emailed back before the turn deadline. The
exact syntax is catalogued in the [Reference](/reference); the common tasks are
covered in the [How-to Guides](/how-to).
