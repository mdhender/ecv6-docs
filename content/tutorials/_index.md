**Tutorials are lessons.** They take you by the hand through a series of steps to
complete a small project, so you can *learn by doing*. Follow them in order — they
assume no prior knowledge of Epimethean Challenge and build on each other.

A tutorial's job is to give you a successful first experience. It is not the place
to look up every option or explain every design decision; for those, see the
[Reference](/reference) and [Explanation](/explanation) sections.
