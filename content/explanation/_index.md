**Explanation is understanding.** These pages discuss and illuminate a topic to
give you a deeper grasp of Epimethean Challenge — its design philosophy, its
science-fantasy setting, and the reasons the systems work the way they do.

Explanation is for study and reflection, away from the keyboard. It provides
context, weighs alternatives, and connects ideas. It does not walk you through a
task; for that, see the [How-to Guides](/how-to).
