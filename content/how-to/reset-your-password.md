This guide shows how to get a new password when your current one has been exposed.
Your password is the shared secret that authenticates your account; see
[Account]({{< relref "/reference/account.md" >}}).

{{< callout type="info" >}}
This guide is a stub. The channel you use to request a reset, and how the new
password reaches you, are placeholders to be filled in for your game.
{{< /callout >}}

## Steps

1. **Request a reset.**
   Ask the GM to reset your password.

   {{< callout type="info" >}}
   State exactly how a player requests a reset for your game here.
   {{< /callout >}}

2. **Receive the new password.**
   The GM reissues your password and sends you the new value. The old password
   stops working once the new one is issued.

3. **Use the new password.**
   Authenticate with the new password from then on.

## Notes

- Resetting your password changes only the password. Your account's **email** and
  your per-game **player ids** are unaffected. See
  [Account]({{< relref "/reference/account.md" >}}) and
  [Players]({{< relref "/reference/players.md" >}}).
- Resetting a password does not change any of your game outcomes: per-player
  randomness is derived from your player id, not your password. See
  [Determinism]({{< relref "/reference/determinism.md" >}}).
