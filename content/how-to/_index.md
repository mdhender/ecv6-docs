**How-to guides are recipes.** They give you directions to achieve a specific,
real-world goal. Unlike tutorials, they assume you already know the basics and
just need the steps to get something done.

Each guide is a self-contained answer to a question of the form *"How do I…?"* —
for example, *"How do I submit my turn by email?"* or *"How do I found a new
colony?"*
