This guide shows how to get your orders for the current turn to the GM before the
deadline.
It assumes you are a player in an active game and have received your report.

{{< callout type="info" >}}
This guide is a stub. The email address, subject-line convention, and deadline
policy are placeholders to be filled in for your game.
{{< /callout >}}

## Steps

1. **Find the current turn.**
   Open your latest [report]({{< relref "/reference/turns.md" >}}); it is for the
   turn you are issuing orders for. Note that turn number.

2. **Write your orders.**
   In a plain-text editor, write one order per line using the syntax in the
   [Orders reference]({{< relref "/reference/orders.md" >}}).
   Do not use a word processor — orders must be plain text.

3. **Address the email.**
   Send the orders to the game's turn address with the subject your game uses to
   identify your empire and the turn number.

   {{< callout type="info" >}}
   Fill in the exact address and subject-line format for your game here.
   {{< /callout >}}

4. **Send before the deadline.**
   Orders that arrive after the GM processes the turn are not applied. Send with
   time to spare.

## Verify

You will see the results of your orders in the **next** turn's report, which
describes the state at the start of that turn. If an order was rejected, the
report (or a reply from the GM) will say so.
