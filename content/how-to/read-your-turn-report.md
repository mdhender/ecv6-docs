## Steps

1. **Confirm the turn.**
   Your report is for a specific turn and describes the game at the **start** of
   that turn, before any of the turn's orders are applied.
   See [Turns]({{< relref "/reference/turns.md" >}}).

2. **Review each section.**
   Read through the report to see what your empire can observe this turn.
